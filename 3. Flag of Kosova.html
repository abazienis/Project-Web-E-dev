Flag of Kosova

The flag of Kosova has yellow, white and blue colors with the map of Kosova in the middle and 6 stars representing 6 communities. On February 17, 2008, together with the independence, the flag of Kosova was chosen by the Parliament.


The designer of the flag is Muhamer Ibrahimi, who is also a former fighter of the Kosova Liberation Army.
