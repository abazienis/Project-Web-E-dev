Republic of Kosova

Republic of Kosova is an independent country located in the Southeast Europe.


Kosova borders Serbia to the north and east, North Macedonia to the southeast, Albania to the southwest, and Montenegro to the west. It has an area of 10,880 km² and a population of 1.8 million inhabitants.


With the declaration of independence on February 17, 2008 the Republic of Kosova became the newest country in the world.


The Republic of Kosova is divided into seven administrative regions:


  • Gjilan region
  • Prishtina region
  • Prizren region
  • Peja region
  • Mitrovica region
  • Ferizaj region
  • Gjakova region
